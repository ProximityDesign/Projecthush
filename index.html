I don't know if I should quit my job.

What's making this decision feel difficult right now?
I'm mentally exhausted. I don't enjoy the work anymore. But the salary is good and everyone thinks leaving is bad.
H
When did you first start feeling disconnected?
Maybe a year ago. Now I genuinely don't want this life long term.
H
What part drains you the most?
Building someone else's dream while ignoring mine.
H
And what is "your dream" that keeps showing up?
Building products. Creative work. Meaningful stuff.
H
If you stay for 2 more years, what feeling comes up?
Trapped.
H
And when you imagine leaving?
Fear… but also relief.
H
What are you truly afraid of losing?
Stability. Approval. My identity as someone "doing well."
H
If nobody judged you, what would you honestly want?
I'd leave. I'd take time to build something of my own.
H
What's the biggest thing stopping you right now?
Money.
H
How long could you survive without this job today?
Around 4 or 5 months.
H
Do you have skills to support freelancing?
Yeah. I've done UI/UX freelance before.
H

  • Prepare for a transition
